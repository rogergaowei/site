Cruise ship highlights (Princess Discovery)

Seven days of cruise highlights aboard Discovery Princess, from Vancouver to Alaska's ports, glaciers, games, and family adventures.

Day one: July 4, 2026 — Port Vancouver

Highlights

  • Boarding the ship Discovery Princess.
  • Playing with my cousin, Ethan: mini golf, jumping on the stones, and playing the Eagle.
  • Exploring the ship, mainly on decks 15–17.

Day two: July 4, 2026 — Sea day

Highlights

  • Showing Ethan my Minecraft world.
  • My entire family playing games. Ethan and I played our own version of Rivals for Catan.
  • Ethan lost his 25¢ coin!

Day three: July 4, 2026 — Port Ketchikan

Highlights

  • Wandering around the city to look for salmon, but there were no salmon.
  • Going to a souvenir shop and buying a refrigerator magnet that showed some of Alaska's cities, including Ketchikan, Juneau, and Utqiagvik.
  • Mom found a T-shirt for Dad that said, “Everything is bigger in Texas … until you're in ALASKA.”

Day four: July 4, 2026 — Port Juneau

Highlights

  • Seeing a glacier at Skagway.
  • Seeing a fish processing plant.
  • Seeing the USS Juneau memorial.

Day five: July 4, 2026 — Port Skagway

Highlights

  • Eating gelato.
  • Seeing the capitol.
  • Playing in the playground close to the capitol.

Day six: July 4, 2026 — Glacier Bay

Highlights

  • Nothing.
  • NOTHING.
  • N.O.T.H.I.N.G.

Day seven: July 4, 2026 — College Fjord

Highlights

  • Basketball.
  • Seeing the Mendenhall Glacier ice calving. I saw the biggest chunk break off!
  • Playing the Eagle.

A 25¢ coin is a quarter.

Day eight was disembarkation day, and I got a 1,000-point end. That was really good because when the cruise journey started, I could barely get a 100-point end! P.S. That means I got better by more than 100 every day!
