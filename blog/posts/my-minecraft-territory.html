My Minecraft Territory

A tour of a giant Minecraft territory with a rollercoaster, cherry tree biome, meat shop, and building techniques.

January 01, 2026

My minecraft territory is enormous and amazing. It has a rollercoaster, a entire man-made cherry tree biome, and a small shop for wandering traders.

The Rollercoaster

My rollercoaster is big, and here is what of it exists:

  • a powered rail section
  • a steep climb
  • a fast, shaky part
  • a small bridge with most portions with slopes
  • a small tunnel with some minecarts on activator rails
  • a slope up that turns into a jump
  • a bigger bridge
  • a turning part
  • a free-fall that goes down to the cherry tree biome

Here's what I'm planning to add:

  • a small slope that leads to a deeper and bigger tunnel that has the same layout as the smaller tunnel
  • a water elevator that leads to the ground a copy of the big bridge
  • a ending section that shoots fireworks with the help of a detector rail, sticky pistons, redstone blocks, dispensers, and of course: fireworks!!!
  • a final end with powered rails and a final activator rail

Fun Fact: Did you know that my rollercoaster is so big that it goes beyond my cherry tree biome? Well, the rollercoaster is so big that it is part of my border!

The cherry tree biome

My cherry tree biome is ginormous, too. I took about two Minecraft days to finish making it. Here are the things I did to make the biome:

1. Removing the trees: this was the hardest part. I had to remove all the trees in the region.

2. Planting the cherry trees: (yay!!!) this was the fun part. I planted cherry samplings all over the cleared ground. When I was sure that a sampling couldn't grow, I destroyed it.

Fun Facts: (1) I'm still destroying trees! (2) Somehow, as I was destroying a sampling, it sprouted and I ended up destroying its trunk!

The meat shop

This meat shop isn't ordinary at all. Here is its appearance:

  • Walls: The walls are made of sea lanterns and heavy cores.
  • Floor: The floor is out of beacons.
  • Roof: The roof is made out of respawning podiums.

Special features:

  • The walls have customized banners hanging from them, item frames showing what is sold, and ender dragon heads hanging on the same layer as the item frames.
  • The stairs are also made out of ender dragon heads.
  • The walls also have a small layer of heavy cores to let light in.

I make the meat from the basement. I have a netherite sword to kill the mobs. To spawn the mobs, I use a redstone clock and a dispenser. The basement also has some furnaces to cook the meat.

My territory is huge, has lots of fun stuff, and a food shop to stock up on food. It is surely the best territory ever!!

Special techniques

Since it is hard to literally place blocks, here are some techniques I used to make my territory:

1. Techniques for the roller coaster

  • If you want to go a long way without falling, losing your roller coaster, or getting lost, I recommend sneaking. Like that, you won't fall.
  • It is kind of a hassle to switch the block you are using when you make your roller coaster in the air. Try to make the frame first, then lay the rails.
  • If you go up or down, don't break the blocks that won't be used. If you have to break the top block, the lower ones keep it going and you don't have the lay the frame again. It is also easier.

2. Techniques for the cherry tree biome

  • You don't have to destroy the leaves on a tree. Once you disconnect all the leaves and trunk, the leaves will gradually disappear.
  • The trees take a long time to grow on their own. You can make them grow instantly by adding bone meal.
  • To make big trees, place 4 samplings next to each other in a square. Just add bone meal to one of them to grow the trees.

3. Techniques for the meat shop

  • Experiment! I got the cool look of my shop by experimenting with a lot of blocks. The one I didn't allow was the vault because it was too scary. Used it in another shop.
  • Use spawn eggs to spawn mobs quickly. The mobs that produce meat are sheep, cows, chickens, pigs, mooshrooms, rabbits, hoglins, cod, and salmon.
  • The best fuel for furnaces is the seaweed block. It can cook 20 items with one block.

My territory is huge. You wanna ride the rollercoaster? Do it with minecarts filled with TNT!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Don't worry. There are no activator rails on the course, so the TNT won't explode.
